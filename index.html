About

I'm a self taught Web Developer currently working at a tech start up. After flirting with the idea of learning to code for years, I decided to pursue it 100% in November 2016 and never looked back.

I got my first tech job back in July 2015, learning the termonology used by developers as well as the workflow. Through countless interactions with our clients and our development team, I've gained the valuable skill of being able to translate our client's needs to our developers.

Working at a tech startup has also given me the opportunity to wear many hats. Often times I find myself managing projects that directly relate to an issue brought up by a client.

Client technical support, product support, project management and now web development.. I've been through it all! While all of those roles have been fun, I'd like to focus my learning on web development. I believe these expereinces will prove to be an asset to any company!

Still not convinced? Check out my linkedin profile (linked in the footer section) for recommendations given to me by past and present colleagues, mentors and supervisors!
